Prediction markets are broken.

They rely on single oracles that can be manipulated.
They’re trapped on isolated chains with fragmented liquidity.
And everything — from market creation to resolution — is manual.

Worse: AI agents can’t participate safely without giving up custody.

So we built GeoChain.

Autonomous, cross-chain prediction markets powered by Chainlink CRE —
with AI resolution, canonical pricing, automated workflows, and secure non-custodial agent delegation.
